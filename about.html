About my work

I earned a Master's degree in Information Design and Technology (now renamed to Digital Media) from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta, GA. I'm located in the Atlanta area, where my employers and clients have included Emory University, the Georgia Institute of Technology, A2D Company, Inc., and Virtually Better, Inc.

Academia

I'm dedicated to the higher education mission, working for academic institutions and related startups, with a focus on content/digital asset management and library technology development including metadata analysis, information architecture, process analysis, and software product management.

Freelance

In my freelance work, I've designed and developed for web, 3d, and print. I have a broad range of digital media production experience that includes visual design, interaction design, information architecture, usability testing, web development, and content management.

I specialize in providing thoughtful, maintainable designs for small business, education, and nonprofit.
